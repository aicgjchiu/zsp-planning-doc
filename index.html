01

Core Loop (one minute read)

01 · Enter

Arrive at map carrying all upgrades from the previous map. Enemies are already scaled to the cumulative vote-upgrades.

02 · Find Portal

Portal spawns at a random tagged point with minimum distance from player spawn. Players must explore to find it.

03 · Optional quests

Light side objectives (kill tags, reach triggers, interact) via the existing Quest System. Skippable.

04 · Kill → Level

On level-up, a 5s invincibility popup appears to pick an upgrade; the world keeps ticking. Weapon determines the upgrade pool.

05 · Activate

Channel 5s on the Portal. Enemy AI pivots to attack the Portal — players defend. On completion: leave or stay to farm.

06 · Farm / Leave

5-minute CD to re-Activate for a 2nd round on the same map, or leave immediately for the next. The Enemy Portal never sleeps.

06

Open Questions

from the pivot deck — still to resolve
Q1 · Portal spawn logic

Fully random? Or region-preference + min-distance? Must avoid spawn-kill — Portal appearing in the middle of an enemy cluster.

Proposal: Weighted tagged points (ATargetPoint), min 80m from player start, at least 1 per map quadrant.

Q2 · 5-minute farm CD

Does the farm window feel pressured (Enemy Portal still absorbing)? Should CD be shortened?

Proposal: Start at 5 min; tune from playtest data — if >60% of groups leave immediately, drop to 3 min.

Q3 · Portal durability balance

HP / defense / respawn time for both Portals need a balance spreadsheet. Prototype first.

Proposal: Player Portal 800 HP (invulnerable until Activate starts); Enemy Portal 1500 HP with 90s respawn elsewhere.

Q4 · Tone preservation

New systems are hardcore-adjacent. How to keep the Party-Game meme vibe?

Proposal: Meme-y item names/SFX, goofy enemy death animations, emotes. Commit to tone in VFX + audio direction.
